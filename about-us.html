ABOUT US

Introducing the dynamic trio behind the awe-inspiring journey of our science museum: Dr. Panida Paethanom, the stargazing luminary lunatic and astrophysicist; Aksel Oldeide, the ingenious engineer weaving innovation into every exhibit; and Helene Høgås, the enthusiastic educator infusing a sense of wonder into science education. Driven by their collective passion for demystifying the marvels of the universe, this visionary alliance has birthed an immersive space where curiosity knows no bounds.
